The Festive 5k has a choose your own route difficulty as it covers 15 indiviual locations spanning Newport. Thefore you can choose to do 10 or 2.

Info on Route

Length is 10.6 km. An easy-going route which is suitable to most abilities, so a wonderful opportunity to get outdoors. Moorhens and other wildlife all along the canal. Nice easy walk and free parking too. There are lots of cyclists as it's a flat route, so best to keep dogs on a lead. This trail is a great place to reconnect with nature.

What does it cost to join in?

Nothing - it's free! but please register before you first come along. Only ever register with parkrun once and don't forget to bring a scannable copy of your barcode (request a reminder). If you forget it, you won't get a time.
